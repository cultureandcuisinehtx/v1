ABOUT US

Culture and Cuisine

We are a group dedicated to providing a space where the people of Houston can begin to learn about the cultures that make-up our community.

Through conversations, we can build an understanding of why others do the things they do rather than build irritation about why they are different.

With every discussion, we begin to open an understanding of what it is to be in another’s world and begin to understand that at our core, we are all human.

Just expressed in a different way.

Culture and Cuisine is a group that meets monthly. The group features 2-3 speakers from a specific culture while attendees enjoy meals typical of the culture. The event helps to create a conversation that will ultimately lead to more understanding, acceptance, and empathy between cultures and work to unite the community of Houston (now one of the most diverse cities in the nation).
